National Scholarships Portal is one-stop solution through which various services
starting from student application, application receipt, processing, sanction and disbursal of various scholarships to Students are enabled.

National Scholarships Portal is taken as Mission Mode Project under National e-Governance Plan (NeGP).

Currently, the NSP has 3 departments and 3 registered schemes.
